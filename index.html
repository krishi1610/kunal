Technical Events

Events

Poster Presentation

The Poster Presentation Event is an individual competition designed to enhance participants' skills in creating and presenting technical posters. Open to all B.E/B.Tech students, participants are required to submit posters on department-specific topics such as Cloud Computing, Signal Processing, Mechanical Engineering Trends, and more. The event focuses on broadening students' knowledge in their field of study, with participants expected to present their research findings before a panel of judges. The competition also emphasizes effective communication skills, critical thinking, and technical expertise in their chosen topic.

Capture the Flag (C T F)

Capture The Flag (CTF) Hackathon organized by the CIS Association, in collaboration with IIT Tirupati. This event is designed for cybersecurity enthusiasts to showcase their skills in solving real-world challenges. The hackathon will consist of three rounds with progressively tougher challenges, and it offers both individual and team participation options.

Code Slayer - Coding Club

Code Slayer is a solo coding competition designed to test your programming skills and problem-solving abilities. Participants will engage in two rounds of challenging activities aimed at enhancing their coding proficiency.

Dev_Era

Dev_Era is an exciting solo and team coding competition designed to test your development skills through two challenging rounds. Participants will engage in activities that promote creativity and technical proficiency. Join us for an exhilarating experience where you can showcase your skills and compete for amazing prizes!

Project Expo

Project Expo is a technical event in which the participants should prepare their project at home and bring the project to demonstrate before the judges. There is only one round, and the best project is chosen by the judges, with respective prizes given away.

Circuit Haunting - Robotics Club

The event aims to assess participants' knowledge and skills in circuit designing, circuit solving, and circuit identification across three levels.

Technical Quiz - Departmental Event

A quiz is a game which can also be called a mind sport wherein the players, either as individuals or in teams attempt to answer questions posed to them correctly, in order to win a prize. We designed these to promote a fun way of study and help in the process of improving one’s general knowledge.

Tower of Strength - Robotics Club

The event is structured as a representation of real-time robots and aims to cover basic information about robots in two levels.

Paper Presentaion - Departmental Event

The Paper Presentation Event is an individual competition designed to enhance participants' skills in creating and presenting technical papers. Open to all B.E/B.Tech students, participants are required to submit papers on department-specific topics such as Cloud Computing, Signal Processing, Mechanical Engineering Trends, and more. The event focuses on broadening students' knowledge in their field of study, with participants expected to present their research findings before a panel of judges. The competition also emphasizes effective communication skills, critical thinking, and technical expertise in their chosen topic.
